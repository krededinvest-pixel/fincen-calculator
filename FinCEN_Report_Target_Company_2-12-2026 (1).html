üìã About FinCEN Reporting Requirements

Corporate Transparency Act (CTA): The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) requires U.S. companies to report beneficial ownership information as part of anti-money laundering efforts.

Beneficial Owner Definition: An individual who, directly or indirectly:

  • Exercises substantial control over the reporting company, OR
  • Owns or controls at least 25% of the ownership interests of the reporting company

What Must Be Reported:

  • Full legal name
  • Date of birth
  • Current residential or business address
  • Unique identifying number from acceptable ID (e.g., driver's license, passport)
  • Image of the identification document

Filing Deadlines:

  • Companies created before 2024: File by January 1, 2025
  • Companies created in 2024: File within 90 days of formation
  • Companies created after 2024: File within 30 days of formation
